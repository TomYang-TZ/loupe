Animation Spec

Light → Dark
  • Sun scales up to 1.75x (0.25s ease)
  • Sun beams rotate -25deg + fade out (0.15s)
  • Moon mask circle moves in (cx 24→17, 0.5s delay 0.25s)
Dark → Light
  • Sun scales down to 1x (0.5s elastic)
  • Sun beams rotate back + fade in (0.5s elastic)
  • Moon mask circle retreats (cx 17→24, 0.25s)

Respects prefers-reduced-motion — all transitions disabled when set to reduce. Icon size: 22px in topbar (both minimal and full mode header). Hover: subtle color shift.
