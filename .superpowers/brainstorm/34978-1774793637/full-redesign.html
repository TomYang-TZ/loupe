Key Design Decisions

Zinc palette, not GitHub dark
Pure black (#09090b) background. Zinc neutrals. Feels like Linear/Raycast, not GitHub. More OLED-friendly, higher contrast.
Floating modal on click
Clicking any entry opens a centered modal with backdrop blur. Esc or click outside to dismiss. No inline expand.
RESULT is quiet
Results use gray border + muted text. Tool name is dimmed. Only USE and THINK are visually prominent. Errors pop in red.
Hover = subtle surface lift
No background color change on hover. Just a subtle surface elevation (#18181b) + border-left brightens slightly. Clean and restrained.
